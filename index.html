About Us

Meet the team.

Who We Are

People who love outdoor activities, hiking or just spending a few days away from cities or classical accommodation and restauration facilities.

What We Do

We offer a variety of alternative accommodation spaces, from standard camping to heated glamping yurts, watchtowers and hammocks for sleeping under the starry sky in a wild natural landscape.

Why Choose Us

Because we provide all the conditions of classical cabin or mountain bungalows, in a large secured space with more freedom of movement than anywhere else. And all this in a location not far from the main access routes and really close to other touristic objectives.
